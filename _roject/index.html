Vision

A premier institute providing quality technical education to rural masses for disseminating fruits of development for welfare of the region and nation.

Mission

  • To cultivate a quality teaching and learning environment.
  • To promote students to contribute for fulfilling the socio-economic needs through appropriate technology.

Goals

  • Set up a conducive environment with modern interactive educational aids.
  • Organize faculty development programs.
  • Enhance career opportunities for students through industry interaction.
  • Build up a network of institute alumni to enhance our reputation with the industry and facilitate job opportunities.
